Introduction to AI and ML in Elasticsearch

Objective

These labs explain why AI and ML are essential for modern search and how Elasticsearch's built-in capabilities dramatically improve search recall and precision.

The Search Challenge

While keyword search is powerful and remains essential, it has some gaps that AI can help fill:

  • Vocabulary mismatch: Users search "car" but documents say "automobile"
  • Limited semantic understanding: Struggles with intent and context beyond exact matches
  • Missed relevant content: May not surface related concepts due to keyword differences

Note: Keyword search excels at exact matching and remains a cornerstone of modern hybrid search approaches.

How AI/ML Enhances Search

  • Semantic Understanding: AI complements keywords by understanding meaning and context
  • Enhanced Recall: Finds relevant content through both keyword matches and semantic similarity
  • Hybrid Precision: Combines keyword relevance with semantic ranking for optimal results

Best results come from combining keyword search with AI - not replacing it!

What You'll Build

In the upcoming labs, you'll implement these AI-powered enhancements:

ELSER Inference Endpoint

Sparse vectors that expand queries with related terms. "Car" finds "automobile," "vehicle," "sedan."

E5 Inference Endpoint

Dense embeddings for multilingual semantic search. Query in English, find Spanish results.

Chat Completions Endpoint

LLM integration for conversational search and natural language responses.

Semantic Reranking Endpoint

Re-orders results using semantic similarity for better ranking accuracy.

ML Models in Elasticsearch

Custom models for entity recognition, classification, and domain-specific tasks.

Recall vs Precision

  • Recall: Did we find all relevant results? (Comprehensiveness)
  • Precision: Are the returned results actually relevant? (Accuracy)

AI models enhance both recall and precision by understanding semantic relationships while preserving the power of exact keyword matching.

The Goal

Transform keyword search into intelligent, hybrid AI-powered search that combines the precision of exact matching with semantic understanding of user intent.
